This isn’t about panic; it’s about keeping our heads while we lock the doors.

    Take 15 minutes: freeze your credit at Equifax, Experian, TransUnion—free, online, done. Next, passwords—“LanturnShines25!” beats “password123” (tips at CISA.gov).     Now, the glow: before you dive in, sit. Five minutes, eyes closed—breathe in for 4, out for 6. Feel your chair, not the what-ifs. NIH says this lifts calm 20%—your brain’s a muscle, give it a breather. Mindful clicks beat frantic ones; you’ll lock in stronger. Done it?
