Hey, Lanturn light-bearers! This week—flu, fire, layoffs—showed what’s at stake: our health, safety, work. We pitched shots, alarms, skills. Hit lanturnweekly.com/glow with a story or pic—your alarm beep, your course badge. Connection’s not soft; it’s steel. Made a link? Tell us—we’re brighter together.
